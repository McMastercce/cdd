Internal Files

The following information is for internal LSTs.

Below you will find details on where internal network share files are located and the purpose that they serve.

Location Purpose
N:\CCE Course Development Internal course development team folder.
N:\CCE Course Development\Courses Within a particular course folder there are course development files and a copy of all of the files as the appear in the Manage Files area of the course in Avenue.
Google Account used for hosting course videos and host the Course Development Hub site.
Contact an LST to get access to these accounts.
